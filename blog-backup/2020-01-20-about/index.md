# Who Am I?

My name is Yochai. I write about RPGs, and related works of fantasy and science fiction. I co-host the [Between Two Cairns](https://patreon.com/betweentwocairns) podcast, and I've made a [few things](https://newschoolrevolution.com/my-stuff). I am the founder and creative director of [Cairn Press](https://cairnpress.ink).
